Welcome To Ngena Ekasi Tours

Ngena Ekasi Tours specializes in township tours, day tours and school outings in Gugulethu Township and other Cape Town Landmarks. Being conveniently located to serve and welcome local and international visitors, we are able to use our vast experience and skills in the tourism industry to provide safe, secure, affordable and informative tours.

  • Ngena Ekasi is registered with Cape Town Tourism and The South African Township and Village Tourism Association (SATOVITO).
  • The company is 100% owned and managed by Thandisizwe (Zozo) Dano, a qualified Clothing Industry Quality Controller who has more than 15 years of experience in the entertainment industry.
  • Thandisizwe's people skills, attention to detail and his ability to service clients professionally and promptly is what sets Ngena Ekasi apart. Mr. Dano is committed, enthusiastic and passionate about developing and growing Ngena Ekasi into a Cape Town favorite.
